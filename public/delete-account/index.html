Listorix

Delete your account and data

You can request deletion of your Listorix account and associated personal data at any time.

Delete from inside the app

  1. Open Listorix.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Choose Account or Settings.
  4. Select Delete account, then confirm.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email support@listorix.com from the email address linked to your Listorix account. Use the subject line “Delete my Listorix account”.

What will be deleted

We will delete or anonymize account information, personal grocery lists, shopping history, profile data, saved app preferences, and other data associated with your Listorix account, unless retention is required for security, fraud prevention, or legal reasons.

How long it takes

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days after verifying the account owner.

Deleting the app from your phone does not automatically delete your Listorix account. Please use the app deletion flow or email us to request deletion.
