Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Listorix collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Listorix mobile app and website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information such as email address and optional display name.
  • Shopping data such as lists, grocery items, prices, quantities, budgets, and shopping history.
  • Device and app information such as device type, operating system, app version, crash diagnostics, and performance data.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide grocery list creation, history, household sharing, insights, and sync features.
  • To maintain your account and restore your data across devices when you sign in.
  • To process receipt scans and voice input features you explicitly choose to use.
  • To improve app stability, performance, and feature quality.
  • To send reminders or notifications if you enable them.

3. Voice, Microphone, Camera, and Photos

  • Microphone access is used only when you explicitly use voice input.
  • Camera and photo library access are used only when you explicitly scan or upload a receipt.
  • Receipt images are processed for text extraction and are not kept longer than necessary for processing.
  • We do not use camera or microphone permissions for advertising or hidden background recording.

4. Storage and Service Providers

Listorix stores some data locally on your device and may sync account data to secure cloud infrastructure operated through Supabase for authentication and database services.

5. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not share your data with advertising networks or data brokers.
  • We only share data with service providers needed to run the app, such as hosting, authentication, and database infrastructure.

6. Retention and Deletion

We keep your data for as long as needed to provide the service or meet legal obligations. When you request deletion of your account, we will delete or anonymize personal data within a reasonable period, subject to any required retention obligations.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information. To request help with your data, contact us using the details below.

8. Children’s Privacy

Listorix is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at support@listorix.com.
