Common questions
How do I delete my account?
Go to the app → Profile → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account". Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days. Or email us and we'll handle it manually.
My data isn't syncing — what should I do?
Sign out and sign back in to refresh your session. If the issue persists, email us with your device model and app version (visible on the Profile screen).
Can I use Listorix without creating an account?
Yes. Your grocery list is saved locally on your device with no account required. Signing in enables cloud sync across devices.
Is there an Android version?
An Android version is in the works. Sign up to be notified when it launches.
